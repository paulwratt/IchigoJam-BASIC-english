IchigoJam PC
-----------------------------------------------------------
[Function]
Specify the line number to be executed next.

[Command format]
GOTO <line number>

[Example]
GOTO 10

[Commentary]
Transfer processing to the line specified by <line number>.
From 0.9.5 it is possible to use calculation formulas, variables, etc. for <line number>.
