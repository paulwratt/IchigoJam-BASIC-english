[Function]
Restart program execution from the stopped line.

[Command format]
CONT

[Example]
CONT

[Commentary]
Stopping program with Esc key,
Or from the program stopped line with the STOP command
We will resume.
CONT is not a continuation from STOP,
Please be careful to execute from the beginning of the stopped line.
