SCROLL
IchigoJam BASIC 0.9.7～
IchigoJam BASIC RPi
IchigoJam PC
-----------------------------------------------------------
[Function]
Scroll up, down, left and right.

[Command format]
SCROLL <direction>

[Example]
SCROLL 2

[Commentary]
Scroll one character up, down, left, or right in one direction.
<direction> is as follows.
UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT are supported from 1.0.0 beta 7.

    0 · UP = up
    1 · RIGHT = right
    2 · DOWN = down
    3 · LEFT = left
